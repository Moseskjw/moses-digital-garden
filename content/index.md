## Gist
The purpose of this digital garden is to document down my thoughts/ processes/ knowledge that I have accumulated throughout different life experiences. It would be a mix of factual and subjective pieces. 

I hope that the ramblings that I share here do find itself to be of value to someone out there. 

## Overview
I have currently chose to divide my ramblings into 3 different sections.
- Life
- Work
- Home

**Life** covers a broad range of topics in general ranging from random life ideologies that i subscribe to and anything under the sun.

**Work** would cover the spectrum of topics ranging between soft and hard skills with the main focus on technical knowledge that I have built over the years as a corporate slave. 

**Home** serves to be a knowledge base as a individual living in the Sunny island of Singapore, how I have navigated the challenges of living my life with my family and the learnings from that.
